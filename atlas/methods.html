Rules before results

How evidence enters the atlas.

The public layer recomputes metrics from canonical normalized rows. Editorial text explains decisions; it cannot change arithmetic.

Model plane

The harness keeps its own wire protocol.

Fugue freezes one provider/model route. Each adapter consumes that route through the protocol its harness actually speaks. A provider is called directly when it offers that protocol; otherwise a local, digest-pinned LiteLLM bridge translates the wire format. Candidate identity does not change.

  1. Resolve once.The immutable candidate and snapshot record provider, model, endpoints, modalities, harness and expected native protocol.
  2. Attest the bridge.Bridged runs fail preflight unless the running container uses the pinned image digest and the exact locked config mounted read-only.

  4. Reconcile identity.The public row is accepted only when run, candidate, task, trial, evaluation call and Agent root agree. Model routing and MCP transport remain separate.

Evidence labels: Runtime attested includes a matching adapter receipt. Snapshot attested proves the locked route and bridge but lacks a row-level receipt. Configured only is retained for historical runs that predate these receipts; it is never presented as packet-level proof.

01

Primary outcome

For SWE-bench, resolution is the official deterministic verifier result. Pass rate is accepted repairs ÷ scored predictions. Complete SWE studies require every planned, applicable prediction to reconcile and score; operational completion is reported separately.

02

Secondary measures

Total cost and tokens are published only when every row is measured. Wall time is the median terminal duration. Turns and tool calls come from verified Agent spans. Recoverable errors and refusals remain counts. Missing values are unavailable, never zero.

03

Evidence tiers

Confirmed
Complete replicated holdout with deterministic paired bootstrap.
Directional
Complete one-attempt controlled grid; raw numerator and denominator only.
Baseline benchmark
Complete capability matrix with no intervention.
Contract evidence
Routing, tool-use or observability canary.
Active / blocked
Visible for context, never ranked and never assigned a winner.
04

Hard task lock

The discovery partition was frozen before execution. Eligible tasks require production and test changes, two to eight touched files, repository diversity, and either lexical mismatch or cross-directory production work.

05

Agent-link contract

Every Agent prediction must reconcile to exactly one native conversation and one invoke_agent root. Public links are included only after Fugue verifies the evaluation relationship. They open authenticated Weave; raw content is never copied here.

06

No composite score

Quality, cost, latency, retrieval and observability answer different questions. Collapsing them into one score would hide the tradeoff and let arbitrary weights determine the winner.

07

Paired memory lift

For each fixed model, harness, task and trial, lift is treatment pass − baseline pass; the reported lift is the mean of those paired deltas. A one-attempt grid shows only its numerator, denominator and raw delta.

08

Replicated uncertainty

Confirmed holdout evidence averages repeated outcomes within each comparison example, then performs 2,000 deterministic paired bootstrap resamples of examples. The atlas reports the 2.5th and 97.5th percentiles as the 95% interval.

09

Retrieval measures

Recall@10 is relevant evidence found in the first 10 results ÷ locked relevant evidence. MRR is the reciprocal rank of the first relevant result, or zero when none is retrieved. Neither substitutes for accepted task resolution.

10

Ordering

Complete experiments sort by evidence tier, then reviewed decision value. Rankings exist only inside one compatible workload and cohort. Active and blocked work receives no rank, winner or confidence language.
